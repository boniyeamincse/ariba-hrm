# Doctor Dashboard

## Role: doctor
## Email: doctor@hospital.com
## Scope: Clinical Portal & OPD/IPD Rounds

### Primary Widgets
- Appointments Today: Daily schedule
- In Consultation: Current patients with doctor
- Pending Investigations: Awaiting test results
- Prescriptions Today: Today's prescriptions written

### Quick Actions
1. OPD Queue: Patient queue for consultation
2. SOAP Editor: Clinical notes and assessment
3. Vitals Entry: Patient vital signs recording
4. Appointments: Schedule management

### Focus Areas
- Clinical Consultations
- Patient Safety
- Evidence-based Prescribing

### Menu Access
- OPD Queue & Consultations
- Patient Records
- Prescriptions
- Lab Results
- Investigations
- Referrals

### Access Level
- Patient clinical data
- Consultation notes (SOAP)
- Prescription issuance
- Investigation ordering
- Patient communication
