# Receptionist Dashboard

## Role: receptionist
## Email: reception@hospital.com
## Scope: Front Desk, OPD Queue & Appointments

### Primary Widgets
- Registrations Today: New patient registrations
- Appointments Today: Today's scheduled appointments
- Queue Waiting: Current waiting patients
- Invoices Today: Billing transactions

### Quick Actions
1. Register Patient: New patient intake
2. Appointments: Book and manage appointments
3. Billing: Generate and track invoices

### Focus Areas
- Front Desk Throughput
- Appointment Flow
- Patient Registration

### Menu Access
- Patient Registration
- Appointments
- OPD Queue
- Billing & Payments
- Patient Search

### Access Level
- Patient registration
- Appointment booking
- Basic billing
- Queue monitoring
- Patient demographics
