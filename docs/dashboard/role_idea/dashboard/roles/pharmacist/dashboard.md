# Pharmacist Dashboard

## Role: pharmacist
## Email: pharmacist@hospital.com
## Scope: Pharmacy Dispensing & Stock Management

### Primary Widgets
- Pharmacy Queue: Pending prescriptions
- Low Stock Items: Below reorder level inventory
- Prescriptions Today: Today's prescriptions
- Revenue Today: Pharmacy sales

### Quick Actions
1. Prescription Queue: Dispense medications
2. Stock Alerts: Inventory management
3. Dispense: Record medication dispensing

### Focus Areas
- Dispense Safety
- Stock Continuity
- Medication Verification

### Menu Access
- Prescription Queue
- Inventory Management
- Drug Database
- Dispensing Records
- Pharmacy Reports

### Access Level
- Prescription dispensing
- Inventory management
- Stock level monitoring
- Drug interactions check
- Billing for pharmacy items
