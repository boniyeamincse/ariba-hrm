# Pharmacist Dashboard

## Menu and Submenu

- Pharmacy Overview
- Prescription Queue
  - Pending Prescriptions
  - Dispensed History
- Inventory and Batches
  - Drug Stock
  - Batch Expiry Alerts
- Purchase and Refill
  - Reorder Requests
  - Vendor Orders

## Permission Role

- `pharmacy.prescription.read`
- `pharmacy.dispense.manage`
- `pharmacy.inventory.manage`
- `pharmacy.purchase.request`

## Capabilities

- Validate and dispense prescriptions.
- Monitor stock levels and expiry risk.
- Trigger refill/purchase workflows.
