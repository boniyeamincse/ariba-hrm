- Finance Overview
- Revenue Cycle
  - Invoice Management
  - Payment Reconciliation
- Claims and Payers

- Expenses and Ledger
  - Expense Approvals

## Permission Role

- `finance.dashboard.read`
- `finance.invoice.manage`
- `finance.claim.manage`
- `finance.ledger.read`

## Capabilities

- Monitor revenue and receivables.
- Manage claims lifecycle with insurers.
- Reconcile accounts and generate reports.
