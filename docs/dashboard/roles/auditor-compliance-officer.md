# Auditor / Compliance Officer Dashboard

## Menu and Submenu

- Compliance Console
- Audit Trails
  - Clinical Access Logs
  - Billing Change Logs
  - User Activity Logs
- Policy Monitoring
  - Access Violations
  - Expired Certifications
- Reporting
  - Compliance Reports
  - Remediation Tracker

## Permission Role

- `compliance.dashboard.read`
- `compliance.audit.read`
- `compliance.violation.read`
- `compliance.report.export`

## Capabilities

- Monitor regulatory and internal policy compliance.
- Investigate abnormal access/change activity.
- Produce audit reports and remediation follow-up.
