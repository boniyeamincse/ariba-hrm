# Receptionist / Front Desk Dashboard

## Menu and Submenu

- Front Desk Board
- Patient Registration
  - New Patient
  - Search Patient
  - Duplicate Check
- Appointments
  - Book Appointment
  - Reschedule
  - Cancel
- Billing Intake
  - Generate Invoice
  - Payment Collection
- Helpdesk
  - Visitor Pass
  - Complaint Log

## Permission Role

- `frontdesk.patient.register`
- `frontdesk.patient.read`
- `frontdesk.appointment.manage`
- `frontdesk.billing.create`

## Capabilities

- Register patients and verify demographics.
- Manage appointment lifecycle.
- Initiate billing and collect front-desk payments.
