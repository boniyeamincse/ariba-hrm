# Nurse Dashboard

## Menu and Submenu

- Nursing Board
- Vitals and Triage
  - Record Vitals
  - Triage Queue
- Medication Administration
  - MAR Sheet
  - Due Medications
- Nursing Notes
  - Shift Notes
  - Care Plan
- Ward Activities
  - Bedside Tasks
  - Escalation Alerts

## Permission Role

- `nurse.vitals.manage`
- `nurse.mar.manage`
- `nurse.notes.manage`
- `nurse.tasks.read`

## Capabilities

- Record and monitor patient vitals.
- Administer medications and update MAR.
- Maintain shift notes and care plans.
