# Patient (Portal User) Dashboard

## Menu and Submenu

- My Health Overview
- Appointments
  - Book Appointment
  - Upcoming Visits
- Medical Records
  - Prescriptions
  - Lab Reports
  - Visit History
- Billing and Insurance
  - My Invoices
  - Claim Status
- Profile and Support
  - Personal Info
  - Help Center

## Permission Role

- `patient.profile.read`
- `patient.appointment.manage`
- `patient.records.read`
- `patient.billing.read`

## Capabilities

- Self-service appointment booking and tracking.
- Access personal medical records and reports.
- View invoices and insurance claim status.
