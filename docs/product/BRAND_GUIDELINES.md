# Brand Guidelines

## Brand Objective

Maintain one consistent MedCore identity globally while allowing controlled local adaptation.

## Core Brand Elements

- Product name usage: MedCore HMS.
- Voice: professional, clear, trustworthy, human.
- UI tone: clinical clarity with warm support cues.

## Visual System

- Primary palette and semantic status colors.
- Typography hierarchy for readability in dense clinical screens.
- Iconography style consistency across modules.

## Localization Boundaries

- Logo lockup remains fixed.
- Local language can be used in headers/subtext.
- Regulatory badges vary by country where required.

## UX Writing Rules

- Keep labels short and actionable.
- Avoid slang and culturally ambiguous phrases.
- Use domain-approved medical terminology.

## Governance

- Brand change requests require product and design review.
- Country-specific variants must remain accessibility compliant.
